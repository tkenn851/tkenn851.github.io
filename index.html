About

We are people on a mission. The Red Sea must GLOW!

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our founder, Crazy Larry, woke up one day with a thought in her mind. "Lets makes the Red Sea glow!" And so she contacted authorities and gained donation money from friends, family, and strangers alike to build a collection of nuclear waste to pour into the Red Sea until it glows! After her unfortunate death, the legacy of gaining donation has been passed down to her two children.

  • March 2011

    A dream in the making

    Crazy Larry's children picked up the pen and signed with the governor of the Red Sea. Mr. Poseidon himself to allow our plan to be fulfilled.

  • December 2012

    Drama in the family

    After one of the children left the company because of a major freight train crash, the other son, whos name is Typhinia, took full control over the establishment and created several campaigns and fundraisers around the globe!

  • July 2014

    Where we are now

    After several gallons of raw toxic sewage has been poured into the Red Sea, 11% of the ocean has began to glow! However, marine biologists are taking more research into why the fish seem to be having allergic reactions to the waste and hope for an answer soon.
